Background Images

When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?

A background image is more for decoration and will not show up on google searches for the background. It is also useful for putting text in front of an image. When you need repeating patterns or textures. Image tags are mostly used for online images and for images that are related to the content being shared.
