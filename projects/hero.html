Rodney Tongi

    When it comes to my hero in life only one name comes to mind, Rodney Tongi. My dad pushed me to be the best I could be in everything I did. I couldn't appreciate what he did for me until now. He would take me out to the beach every weekend to train and run. All the success I had, I owe it to my father. You may ask why choose my father as my hero? It is because he is the only person I want to be like when I grow up.
