Our Story

Tongi Snacks is a family-run baking business inspired by the flavors of the Pacific Islands and Mexico. We bring a unique blend of cultures into every bite, combining tradition with creativity.

We started off in Idaho where we had the name Tongi Snacks but instead of baking we made gift baskets upon request.

Why We Bake

My wife started baking as a way to bring comfort and joy to our home, and soon friends and family wanted to order her treats. From tres leches to churro bites, every snack is made with love.

Our Mission

At Tongi Snacks, our mission is simple — to spread happiness through flavor. We believe in using quality ingredients, honoring our heritage, and treating every customer like family. Whether it’s for a wedding, birthday, or just because, we put care into every order.

What Makes Us Different

Our snacks are more than just delicious — they tell a story. Each recipe blends island roots and Mexican flair, creating something truly one-of-a-kind. We make everything in small batches and focus on freshness, flavor, and presentation.

Looking Ahead

As we grow, we’re excited to expand our menu, explore new event spaces, and maybe even open a storefront one day! We’re thankful for the support of our community and hope to keep bringing smiles to tables near and far.

“Every dessert has a story, and we’re honored to be part of yours.” – The Tongi Family
