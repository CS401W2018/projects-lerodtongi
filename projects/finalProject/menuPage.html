Allergy & Ingredients Disclaimer

Our treats are made in a kitchen that handles common allergens, including:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Wheat (Gluten)
  • Tree Nuts
  • Peanuts
  • Soy

We take care to avoid cross-contamination, but we cannot guarantee that any product is completely allergen-free. If you or your guests have any food allergies or dietary restrictions, please let us know when placing your order.
